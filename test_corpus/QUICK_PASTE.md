## How to test

1. Open Chrome with Subtext installed
2. Open the side panel (click ✦ Subtext icon → Open side panel)
3. Paste any text above into the textarea
4. Click **Analyze** or press Cmd+Enter
5. Watch the BS score count up 🎯
